Kevin J. Hart

As the Founder and CEO of Aireal, Kevin Hart has created Aireal's Patented Intellectual Property, lead business operations (including fundraising, business development and planning, ideation, marketing and project management). In this role, Kevin has also provided the necessary insight on user experience design and human sciences to help Aireal become the standard for the future of UX/UI design in Augmented Reality.

Aireal is an Augmented Reality platform that supports the placement of custom content (2D graphics, 3D graphics, Video & Interactive applications) based on predefined longitude, latitude and altitude coordinates. The augmented content then provides a new experience within an existing environment and also accumulates valuable market engagement and human interaction data. Aireal has created a new, ubiquitous world of opportunity, that could not only be seen but also interacted with through the use of mobile and wearable technology.

Aireal prides itself on being a user experience focused medium that provides users infinite variability to environments they are often well aware of. By creating a digital medium with digital content that is anchored to latitude, longitude, altitude coordinates and is only visible through the eyes of technology, we provide brands with a new form of Transmedia story telling and developers a chance to make applications that disrupt the pattern of our day-to-day interaction with the real world.
